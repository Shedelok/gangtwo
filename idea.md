# Ideas

## General

- Get rid of spec duplication ("copy-paste") in the description of the list of the addons in lobby and in the game.
- Black & Red addon should actually make 2 pairs of suits look the same
- Restart should work similar to "move to next round", all players must press it to restart
- "Guess..." addons should force players to "guess" so that they don't forget about this addon
- "Stop the game" should keep the addons code instead of resetting it to the default one.
- "Stop the game" should pre-type the previous nickname into the field for the new one.
- Hovering over an addon description should remove it (so that going up to down with your cursor touches all addons)

## Addons

1. For each round there's a timer (like 1m for example) after which all chips on the table are assigned automatically
   and
   the end of the round is forced
2. Player can only steal from neighbors (the person next to them either on left or right)
3. Each player starts with a counter (for example, 10) each time they steal from another player the counter is
   decreased.
   Once the counter becomes zero, the player can't still anymore, they can only take from the middle of the table.
4. Additional card on flop
5. Additional card on turn
6. Additional card on river
7. Everyone has 3 pocket cards instead of 2
8. After the turn is over, its card is randomly rerolled
9. After the flop is over, a random card from it is rerolled
10. One player doesn't see one of their cards
11. You can see all other players' pocket hands, but you can't see yours
12. At preflop you give one of your cards to the person to your left
13. At preflop you look at your cards and then all hands are randomly shuffled and redistributed.
14. Somewhere in the middle of the game the hand rankings are random shuffled, shown to all players and these new
    rankings
    are used for the rest of the game.
15. You get 3 cards on preflop, but on flop you have to discard one of them.
16. One player once can swap one of their cards with one of the common cards.
17. One player once can show one of their cards to another player
18. Jocker X: X is a random card. One player can once change on of their cards to X that would have no suit.
